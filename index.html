The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo

The Story of Evelyn Hugo and her seven husbands was compelling and well laid out. The story explains the life of Evelyn Hugo a young aspiring actress and her journey through Hollywood fame explained through the stories of all seven of her marriages.

Evelyn is telling the story of her exotic love life to a journalist who is looking for a big break in the company she is working for. Through the novel, the journalist questions why Evelyn specifically chose her to write the biography of her life.

The story contains a major plot twist and has many love triangles, drama, and explores the ugly truth of Hollywood behind the scenes.
